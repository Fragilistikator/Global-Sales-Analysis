Global Sales Analysis

Overview

This project analyzes global sales data using Python, Pandas, Jupyter Notebook, and Tableau to identify trends in revenue, profitability, discounts, and regional performance.

Objectives

* Explore sales and profit performance across regions and product categories.
* Identify factors that impact profitability.
* Investigate the relationship between discounts and profit.
* Create an interactive dashboard to communicate key business insights.

Key Findings

* Furniture generated strong sales but had the lowest profit margin among categories.
* Southeast Asia showed negative profitability and higher average discount rates.
* Discounts above approximately 25–30% were associated with declining profitability.
* Sales and profits peaked during Q4, indicating strong seasonal demand.

Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Jupyter Notebook
* Tableau

Dashboard

The dashboard includes:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Average Discount
* Total Orders
* Sales by Region
* Profit Margin by Category
* Monthly Sales Trends
* Discount vs Profit Analysis
